About me

I received a B.S. degree in automation from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2014, and a Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from City University of Hong Kong, China, in 2020. I was with the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, as a Postdoctoral Researcher. Now, I am currently an Assistant Professor in Center for Advanced Control and Smart Operations (CACSO), Nanjing University.

Research Interests

  • Robust PID control.

  • Time-Delay systems.

  • Networked control and estimation.

  • Security over cyber-physical systems.

  • Phase theory over large-scale networks.

Recent News

Our paper "Mean-Square Stability Radii for Stochastic Robustness Analysis: A Frequency-Domain Approach" has been accepted by IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control as a Full Paper.
