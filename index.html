About me

I received a B.S. degree in automation from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University in 2014, and a Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from City University of Hong Kong in 2020. I was with the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, as a Postdoctoral Researcher. I am currently an Associate Professor at the Center for Advanced Control and Smart Operations (CACSO), Nanjing University.


Research Interests

  • Cross of control and learning.

  • Networked control systems.

  • Phase theory over large-scale networks.

  • Security over cyber-physical systems.

  • Time-Delay systems.

  • Robust PID control.
